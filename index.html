ABOUT



God accidentally spills the heaven's potion onto the earth and the angels didn't take it back.
Accompanied by Tun-AToon - her loyal dog, Mel - the angel starts her journey to the 33 wonders of Indonesia.
These three interconnected collections are classed by rarity and stored as ERC-721 tokens on the Polygon blockchain.
Angel’s Project was initiated by Rumah Harapan and Melanie Subono – an Indonesian famous artist that also known as activist that has made many social movements to help others in need. This formed Angel’s project not just as collectible art but also “social movement" as an identity. By owning Angel’s Project NFTs, you can participate to help people, nature and animals in need through Rumah Harapan.
